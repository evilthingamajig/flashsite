Donate Now: Make a Difference Today

Make a Difference Today

Your donation to Flash Forward directly supports underserved communities with the vital energy poverty and solar lighting equipment they need to save lives. Every contribution, no matter the size, helps provide essential tools and training for volunteer engineers, ensuring better recipient care and improved health outcomes.

Your financial contribution helps us build, deliver, and maintain the solar-powered lights we send to students living without reliable electricity. Every dollar you give is a step toward a brighter future for the kids who receive them.

Together, we can make a tangible difference.
Donate now and join us in Flash Forward.
